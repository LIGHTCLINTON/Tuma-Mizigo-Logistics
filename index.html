Who is Tuma Mizigo?

Tuma Mizigo Logistics is your trusted partner for seamless global shipping solutions. We specialize in reliable import services from the USA, China, Hong Kong, India, and UK to Kenya, ensuring fast and secure delivery of your goods. Additionally, our export services connect Kenya to destinations worldwide, offering efficient logistics for businesses and individuals. With a focus on affordability, transparency, and customer satisfaction, Tuma Mizigo delivers your cargo with care and precision.
Inbound shipments(from abroad to Kenya), take 7-14 working days depending on the origin. We deliver within 1-5 business days for outbound shipments depending on the destination. We do delivery & pickups from your home/business address in Kenya every day from 9am-5:30pm.
